# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Community Governance Directive

---

# Directive Purpose

This directive defines the mandatory business rules governing community participation, networking, discussions, moderation, content management, and professional conduct within the Colaberry Nexus platform.

All community-related functionality must comply with this directive.

Applies to:

- Community Portal
- Alumni Portal
- Institution Staff Portal
- Community Moderation Tools
- Reporting Systems
- Analytics Systems
- AI Agents
