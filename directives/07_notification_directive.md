# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Notification and Communication Governance Directive

---

# Directive Purpose

This directive defines the mandatory business rules governing all platform-generated communications and notifications.

All notification functionality must comply with this directive.

Applies to:

- Notification Engine
- Notification Agent
- Email Delivery Systems
- SMS Delivery Systems
- Push Notification Systems
- In-App Notification Systems
- Reporting Systems
- Administrative Tools
