# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Artificial Intelligence Governance Directive

---

# Directive Purpose

This directive defines the mandatory governance rules for all Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Recommendation Systems, Forecasting Systems, Analytics Systems, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI Agents within the Colaberry Nexus platform.

This is the authoritative AI policy for the platform.

All AI-related components must comply with this directive.
