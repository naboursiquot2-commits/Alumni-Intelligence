# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Jobs and Career Outcomes Governance Directive

---

# Directive Purpose

This directive defines the mandatory business rules governing job opportunities, job postings, applications, career outcome tracking, employer information, and career-related recommendations within the Colaberry Nexus platform.

All jobs and career-related functionality must comply with this directive.

Applies to:

- Alumni Portal
- Institution Staff Portal
- Jobs Module
- Reporting Systems
- Recommendation Systems
- Analytics Systems
- AI Agents

---

Job Listings
