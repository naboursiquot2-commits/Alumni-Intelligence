# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Reporting and Institutional Intelligence Governance Directive

---

# Directive Purpose

This directive defines the mandatory business rules governing reporting, dashboards, analytics, KPI management, institutional intelligence, forecasting outputs, exports, and executive reporting.

All reporting-related functionality must comply with this directive.

Applies to:

- Reporting Module
- Dashboards
- Analytics Systems
- AI Analytics Services
- Reporting Agent
- Institution Staff Portal
- Administrative Portal
