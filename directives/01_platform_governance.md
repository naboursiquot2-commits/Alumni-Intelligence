# Directive Purpose

This document is the highest-level governance directive for the Colaberry Nexus platform.

All agents, services, workflows, integrations, AI systems, execution engines, reporting systems, and future platform capabilities must comply with this directive.

This document functions as the operational constitution of the platform.
