# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Recommendation Governance Directive

---

# Directive Purpose

This directive defines the mandatory business rules governing all recommendation systems within the Colaberry Nexus platform.

It establishes governance for:

- Mentor recommendations
- Job recommendations
- Alumni connection recommendations
- Recommendation scoring
- Confidence scoring
- Recommendation explainability
- Recommendation lifecycle management
- Recommendation auditing
- Recommendation fairness

All recommendation-related platform components must comply with this directive.
