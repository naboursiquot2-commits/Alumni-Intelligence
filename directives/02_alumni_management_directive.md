# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Alumni Management Directive

---

# Directive Purpose

This directive defines the authoritative business rules governing alumni profile management, career history management, skills management, profile visibility, directory participation, and profile ownership.

All platform components that interact with alumni data must comply with this directive.

Applies to:

- Alumni Portal
- Institution Staff Portal
- Reporting Systems
- Recommendation Systems
- Analytics Systems
- Integration Systems
- AI Agents
