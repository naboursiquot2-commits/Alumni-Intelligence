# Colaberry Nexus AI Alumni Intelligence Platform
## Service Extraction & Architectural Evolution Strategy

---

# Document Purpose

This document defines the long-term strategy for evolving the Colaberry Nexus platform from a modular monolith into selectively extracted services.

The objective is to:

- Preserve simplicity early
- Enable scalability later
- Avoid premature microservices
- Maintain governance
- Maintain auditability
- Support future growth

This document serves as the authoritative service extraction roadmap.
